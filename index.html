Puheng Li (李朴恒)

Ph.D. Student

Stanford

I'm a third-year PhD student in the Department of Statistics at Stanford University, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Emmanuel Candès and Prof. Renyuan Xu. My research interest lies broadly in leveraging statistical methods to guide modern machine learning and make them more interpretable, reliable, efficient and accurate with statistical guarantees.

Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in statistics from School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University. I had the pleasure of working with Prof. Lei Wu, Prof. Weijie Su, Prof. Linjun Zhang, and Prof. James Zou during my undergraduate study. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research!
