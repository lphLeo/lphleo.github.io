Welcome!

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Department of Statistics at Stanford University. My research interest lies broadly in leveraging statistical methods to guide modern machine learning and make them more reliable and accurate with statistical guarantees. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in statistics from School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University, where I had the pleasure to be advised by Prof. Lei Wu. I was also very fortunate to have worked with Prof. Weijie Su, Prof. Linjun Zhang, and Prof. James Zou during my undergraduate study. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research!
